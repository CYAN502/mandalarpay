Mandalar Pay business has been created especially with you in mind.

May 24, 2023 • By Mandalar Pay

Created with business in mine. Mandalar Pay enables businesses to grow and prosper by offering cashless recharge option, real-time notifications and secure app that effortlessly combines lifestyle and financial needs.

Dear parents, let me introduce you to our Mandalar Pay mobile application.. Nowadays, the digital era has come to the mobile era, so you can carry out your daily activities from a mobile phone comfortably and quickly..

That's why our Mandalar Pay software can be used every day to fill up phone bills, purchase data plans, etc. Paying various bills, buying airline tickets, We have created and introduced various services such as being able to transfer money to each other for free.

By using Mandalar Pay, you can access the above services from a phone at a low price. For example, you can buy a 1,000 phone bill for 950 kyat. There are 3 ways to work with Mandalar Pay.

No(1) Users = Users who have installed Mandalar Pay Software on their phone and bought it themselves. Those are the people who want to buy and spend their daily needs at a low price for themselves and their families.

No (2) Retailers = Those who install Mandalar Pay Software on their phone (or their shop) and sell products to users directly for profit, such as phone bills, data, airline tickets, etc.

No (3) Agents = Add Mandalar Pay Software to their phones (or stores) and not only sell products directly, but also open Mandalar Pay "users" and "retailers" accounts. Mandalar Pay unit resellers. For those, Mandalar Pay will give extra agents commission.

Agents commissions will not be given at the time of purchase, but commissions will enter users' phones when they use it. Therefore, agents will increase their income by opening more accounts and selling units to users and retailers under their supervision.

So, increase your daily income by partnering with Mandalar Pay. Let all of you be satisfied with the peace of mind and the prosperity of your business.
